Nobody
writes
like
Carmine Vile.

Nobody.

He Wept.

Original
fiction
that
bleeds.

Poignant.

Provocative.

Profane.

Authentic.

Urgent.

Explicit.

Real.

A torrent of tears
takes one to
the brink
where anything
is possible.

Shout it out the window.

Scrawl it on the wall.

Tattoo it over your heart.

He Wept.
