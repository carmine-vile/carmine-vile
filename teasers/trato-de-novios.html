Trato de Novios.

HOW FAR WILL YOU GO TO GET WHAT YOU REALLY WANT?

This is meant to shake you up.

Dark. And way out of bounds.

Trato de Novios—a man and a woman find each other and fall in love.

Now, what could go wrong with that?

Let me tell you.

Put your finger on it. That moment when somebody matters more than he did before, more than she should ever. Something's there that wasn't. You can taste it, almost. And you want more. You want it to never go away.

The sun is the same. The breeze. The sound of the traffic. But they're not. Even the garbage truck is shiny and new.

Did you notice the weeds growing in the cracks of the sidewalk? Hope made a cameo today.

It boils down to this. Transactions help us keep our distance. They keep us safe. They make us blind to what's on the other side.

There's only one problem.

This man and this woman—they meet in a brothel. A massage parlour. Yeah, those things are legal in Mexico.

Is that a problem? Why? Why is it a problem?

And if it's not—why not? How can it not be?
